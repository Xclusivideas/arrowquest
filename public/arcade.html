    Game Objective

    Shoot arrows at falling fruits to score points. Avoid hitting bombs!

    Controls

    • Use arrow keys to move the archer
    • Move left/right to change facing direction
    • Click or tap to shoot an arrow
    • Mouse position controls vertical aim

    Scoring

    • Red Apple: 10 points
    • Green Pear: 20 points
    • Golden Star Fruit: 50 points
    • Combo multipliers for consecutive hits!

    Tips

    • You have 20 arrows - use them wisely!
    • Game ends when time runs out, all arrows are used, or a bomb is hit
    • Higher fruit = higher score multiplier
